Blood Borne Pathogens Prediction
The AFI Pathogen Prediction System is a web-based application that uses artificial intelligence to predict the presence of pathogens in clinical samples. The system is powered by a large dataset of patient records and pathogen data, and it is able to provide accurate predictions in a matter of seconds. The AFI Pathogen Prediction System can help clinicians to make better decisions about patient care, and it can help to improve the overall outcomes of patients with infectious diseases.
